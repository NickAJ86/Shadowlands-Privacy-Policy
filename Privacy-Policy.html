Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 11, 2026

App name: Shadowlands

Overview

Shadowlands is an offline tabletop RPG companion tool. Your privacy is important to us—this app is designed to collect no personal data whatsoever.

Data Collection

This app does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information. Specifically:

  • No personal data is collected (name, email, location, etc.)
  • No usage analytics or tracking of any kind
  • No cookies or advertising identifiers
  • No third-party analytics SDKs or ad networks
  • No accounts or sign-up required

Local Storage

All app data (characters, maps, session notes, preferences) is stored locally on your device only using your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Internet Access

The app requests the INTERNET permission solely for loading the application shell via Capacitor. No user data is transmitted over the network.

Third-Party Services

This app does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, advertising networks, or social media SDKs.

Children's Privacy

This app does not collect any data from anyone, including children under the age of 13. It is safe for use by all ages.

Advertisements

This app contains no advertisements and never will.

In-App Purchases & Subscriptions

This app contains no in-app purchases or subscriptions. It is a one-time purchase.

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it at any time by clearing the app's data through your device settings or by uninstalling the app.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the revised version will be posted here with an updated effective date.
